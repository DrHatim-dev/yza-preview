What Fits in a YZA Basket Bag?

A bag can catch the light beautifully and still leave you wondering at the door - will it actually hold my day? Ours are woven by hand here in Guéliz, banana leaf and raffia coaxed into shape over roughly 48 hours. So let me show you what slips inside each one - from the little evening size to the one that goes to the sea - the way you would pack it yourself, before it ever reaches your shoulder.

Quick answer

The XS holds the evening - phone, a cardholder, keys, a lipstick, nothing heavy. The S carries the whole ordinary day - wallet, sunglasses in their case, a little pouch, sunscreen. The M is the one that travels - a pareo, a book, a water bottle, a scarf for the breeze. Choose by the day in front of you, not by the centimetre.

Why we count the day, not the litres

In the atelier we never speak of a bag in litres. We think instead of the day it will keep you company through - a morning weaving past the spice stalls, a long lunch under the fig shade, the evening when you want your hands free for everything else. Each one is woven here in Guéliz, banana leaf and raffia worked over roughly 48 hours across six unhurried days, so it reaches you already warm with a little of this city. Knowing what fits, then, is simply the kindest way to choose well. Rooted in Marrakech, made for wherever you carry it.

The XS - your evening essentials

The XS is the one that finishes a look without a word. A phone, a cardholder, the house keys, a lipstick, sunglasses tucked in bare without their case. It was never meant to hold everything - it is for the night you want to carry almost nothing and feel lighter for it. Small, deliberate, the colour of late sun.

The S - your everyday carry

The S is the one my own hand reaches for most mornings. Phone, wallet, keys, sunglasses safe in their case, a small pouch, a little sunscreen - the whole ordinary day, held lightly on the shoulder. It lives in the space between beautiful and useful and never makes you pick one over the other. Easy, honest, the one for every day.

The M - market, seaside, family lunch

The M is the one that keeps pace with a moving day. A folded pareo, a book, a pouch, sunglasses, a water bottle, sunscreen, a thin scarf for the breeze that lifts off the water in the afternoon. Market at first light, the sea by noon, a long family lunch after - it carries the lot without complaint. Roomy, sun-warmed, generous.

Why we show you, not just measure

The measurements are there, of course - but a number on a screen rarely tells you how a bag will sit against your hip. So instead we photograph what fits: real things laid out on a real morning, packed the way your own hands would pack them. Because what guides us here is not speed but intention. Movement over measurement, always.
