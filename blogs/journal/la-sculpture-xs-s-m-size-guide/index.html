La Sculpture XS, S or M: Which YZA Basket Bag Size Is Right for You?

When a sister stands at our table on rue Yougoslavie, turning a basket in her hands, the question is almost always the same - which size do I take home? La Sculpture is woven in three, XS, S and M, and not one of them is a shrunk or stretched copy of the others. Each was shaped for a different kind of day. Let me walk you through them the way I would across the studio table - what each one holds, and the hour it was made for.

Quick answer

Take the XS for evenings and the days you carry almost nothing. The S for everyday - it is the one most sisters reach for first. The M when you are travelling, walking the medina, or heading down to the sea. Same hands, same banana leaf and raffia, the same six days of weaving. Only the rhythm of the day changes.

Three sizes, three kinds of day

In our Guéliz atelier, the women weave each La Sculpture from banana leaf and raffia, by hand, in runs of about fifteen. One basket holds roughly forty-eight hours of work, spread across six days - so when we offer three sizes, it is never to multiply the same bag. XS, S and M each answer a different kind of day. The body, the edge, the handle, the way the strap finds your shoulder - all of it shifts with the scale, and so does the way the bag sits against you as you move. To choose your size is to choose your rhythm.

XS - for evenings and light days

The XS is the small one with the loudest character - compact, sure of itself, made for the days you carry almost nothing. A phone, a key, a lipstick, a folded note. I reach for it at dinners, at summer weddings, with a long dress and bare arms, on a slow walk through Guéliz as the light turns amber. It comes in Hot Red, a small run of about fifteen. Clip on a fruit charm Fatima crochets by hand and it becomes a little joy swinging at your hip. Light, spirited, sun-struck.

S - your everyday companion

When a sister asks me where to begin, I send her to the S. It carries the presence of a true handbag and still folds into ordinary life - phone, wallet, sunglasses, a thin scarf knotted at the strap - without tipping over into beach scale. We weave it in Deep Violet, again a run of about fifteen. This is the one that becomes a habit before you notice: morning errands, an afternoon café, dinner, and the same bag on your arm through all of it.

M - for travel, market and beach

The M is the generous one. It opens for a pareo, a water bottle, a paperback, a small pouch - whatever the day decides to ask of you. This is the bag for the souk at midday, for the long train south, for the sand. Woven in Black Olive, a run of about fifteen, it holds more without losing the line, and it carries exactly the same handwork - only with room to breathe. Roomy, unhurried, warmed through by the sun.

What actually fits inside

Numbers on a screen never quite tell you how a bag lives against the body. So picture it instead: a phone, a wallet, sunglasses, a tube of sunscreen, a thin scarf. The XS keeps you to the essentials and nothing more. The S holds a full everyday with ease. The M takes a whole beach afternoon and asks for nothing back. Across the rest of the wardrobe our fits run free from XS to XXL too - because here, from the first stitch, it has been movement over measurement.

Come and feel the weave

A postcard from Marrakech, made to be carried everywhere. Choose the size that matches your day below, or come to 66 rue Yougoslavie - open noon to eight, closed Tuesdays - and hold all three under the studio light, weigh them on your arm, before you decide.
